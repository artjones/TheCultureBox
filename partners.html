Partner tiers

Standard Partner (Fee)

Flat monthly/quarterly fee for platform presence and member access. Best for partners testing the channel.

  • Listing in Local Perks
  • Member redemption workflow (QR / code)
  • Perk promotion (partner-defined)
  • Optional event collaboration
DMV requires verified licensing for cannabis partners. Texas market uses hemp/culture partners where permitted.

Premium Partner (Fee + Split)

Featured placement + deeper integration. Includes a split on tracked incremental sales tied to Culture Box redemptions.

  • Featured placement + priority visibility
  • Exclusive drops / member nights
  • Higher-tier member access
  • Co-branded campaigns
  • Tracked redemptions (QR/codes)
Split is negotiated and applies only to incremental sales we can track via member redemptions.

What we don’t do

The Culture Box does not sell or ship cannabis. Partners handle product, pricing, payment, taxes, and compliance. We handle membership, curation, and community.
